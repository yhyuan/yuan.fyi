#### Contextual Keywords

These words have special meaning in certain contexts but are not reserved words:

64. `of` (used in for...of loops)
65. `asserts` (used in type assertions)
66. `infer` (used in conditional types)
67. `keyof` (used to get the union of keys from a type)

#### Types

While not strictly keywords, these are built-in types in TypeScript:

68. `boolean`
69. `number`
70. `string`
71. `symbol`
72. `object`
73. `undefined`
74. `bigint`

Note: Some keywords like `any`, `unknown`, `never`, etc., are both types and keywords in TypeScript
